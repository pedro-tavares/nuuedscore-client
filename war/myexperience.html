Jane Taff, Owner
Taff Enterprises LLC


As an educational practitioner, school leader and entrepreneur I have witnessed and heard countless students say over and over again "I don't know". However this did not affect engagement, as their passion burst through the screen. Coach Derrick’s energy was infectious. He demanded for our youth to not only seize the day, but to be excited to be alive. Coach Jane was a constant anchor of knowledge and wisdom covering subjects from Resume building, to money management. It was obvious how much thought and work was put into adapting this curriculum to be engaging and relevant. I highly recommend NuuEDScore and was honored to be in the room learning new teaching techniques. Thank you NuuEDScore.

Program Coordinator
San Francisco Recreation and Parks
