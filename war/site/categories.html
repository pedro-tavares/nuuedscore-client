CATEGORIES WE OFFER

Personality Assessments

The measurement of personal characteristics. These assessmentsare an end result of gathering information intended to advance psychological theory and research and to increase the probabilitythat wise decisions will be made in applied settings.

Resourcing and Matching

Proper resourcing is a key to success. NuuEDSCORE helps in this area with our robust recommendations engine that identifies such resources as tutors, recruiters, colleges, job listings and more.

Learning Style Assessments

The measurement of a person’s preferential way to learn and how that person absorbs, processes, comprehends and retains information. Learners can use recognition of their individual learning styles to find what study methods, environments, and activities help them learn best.

Auto Onboarding

NuuEDSCORE onboarding helps employers and people discover how their roles fit within the company's workforce. NuuEDSCORE addresses an employers need to improve productivity, employee engagement and employee retention helping employees know one another better and how to work together and communicate effectively.

Career Assessments

Are designed to help individuals understand how a variety of personal attributes (i.e., data values, preferences, motivations, aptitudes and skills), impact their potential success and satisfaction with different career options and work environments.

Skills Development Training

This training improves current or future employee performance by increasing an employee’s ability to perform through learning, usually by changing the employee’s attitude or increasing his or her skills and knowledge.

Skills Correlations (Matching)

These are the skills results based from the Personality, Learning and Career assessments. The results identfies a persons strong and weak skills. Custom tailored skills results are provided to the learner based off of these matching correlations.

Entrepreneurship Skills

A broad range of skills such as technical skills, leadership skills and busines management and creative thinking can lend to independent success and entreprenuership. Suc skill can be applied to many different roles within an organization or applied to self-directed business enterpriese.

Improve Reading

We offer custom tailored content designed to effectively improve reading levels for both your personal and professional life which can increase your overall enjoyment of reading and contribute to your path to success.

Resume Building (Coming Soon)

Financial Literacy (Coming Soon)
